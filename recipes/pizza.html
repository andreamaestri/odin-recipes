Margherita Pizza

Description

Enjoy the simplicity of a Margherita pizza with its thin crust, fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and aromatic basil leaves. This classic Italian favorite is a burst of flavors in every bite!

Savor the taste of Italy with this authentic Margherita pizza recipe!



Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste


Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella and tomato on top of the sauce.
  6. Bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  9. Slice and serve hot!
