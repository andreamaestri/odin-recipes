Greek Salad

Description

Experience the vibrant flavors of Greece with this fresh and colorful Greek salad. Made with crisp vegetables, briny olives, creamy feta cheese, and a tangy dressing, it's a taste of the Mediterranean!

Embark on a culinary journey to Greece with this refreshing Greek salad recipe!



Ingredients

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste


Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and Kalamata olives.
  2. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and chopped parsley over the vegetables.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Refrigerate for about 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
